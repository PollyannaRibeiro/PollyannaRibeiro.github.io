About

Curiosity, creativity and readiness to learn are important points that make me what I am, someone that is always ready to learn about new technologies, to make art from colours, vectors and images. I'm graduated in Brazilian Law and, living in London, I decided to go back to my first career option, design. Design was my first university course, studying for 2 years, learned about product, fashion, and history of design, craft, sustainability and ergonomics. I had worked in e-commerce and digital market for sometimes. Since last year, I have been deepening my knowledge in web design and in this year, I decided to focus and to study to become a Fron-End Web Developer.

Projects
